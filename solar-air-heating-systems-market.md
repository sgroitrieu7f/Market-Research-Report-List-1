<p><p>Solar air heating systems can be categorized into active and passive market types. Active solar heating systems utilize mechanical components, such as fans and pumps, to circulate air heated by solar collectors, providing efficient heating for buildings. In contrast, passive solar heating systems rely on building design and materials to naturally absorb and distribute solar energy, enhancing interior warmth without mechanical assistance. Both methods leverage solar energy but cater to different user needs and efficiency preferences within the market.</p></p>
