<p><strong>The Shoulder Replacement Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Anatomical Shoulder Prosthesis</li><li>Reverse Shoulder Prosthesis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The shoulder replacement devices market comprises two primary types: anatomical shoulder prosthesis and reverse shoulder prosthesis. Anatomical shoulder prosthesis closely mimics the natural anatomy of the shoulder, focusing on preserving rotator cuff function, making it suitable for patients with arthritis or other degenerative conditions. In contrast, reverse shoulder prosthesis alters the joint's mechanics, positioning the ball on the shoulder blade and the cup on the arm, which is ideal for patients with rotator cuff tears or severe joint damage.</p></p>
