<p><p>The Fuel Cell Forklift market is primarily divided into three types: PEMFC (Proton Exchange Membrane Fuel Cell) forklifts, DMFC (Direct Methanol Fuel Cell) forklifts, and others. PEMFC forklifts are widely used for their efficiency and quick refueling capabilities, making them ideal for indoor applications. DMFC forklifts leverage methanol as fuel, offering easier storage and handling but typically with lower power density. The "Others" category may include emerging fuel cell technologies and less common applications, broadening market options.</p></p>
