<p><ul><li>CIB/PIM</li><li>IPM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive IGBT market is segmented into two main types: CIB (Converter Integrated Battery) or PIM (Power Integrated Module) and IPM (Intelligent Power Module). CIB/PIM integrates power electronic functions within the battery system, enhancing efficiency and reducing space requirements. In contrast, IPMs are designed for controlling electric motors, incorporating driving circuits and protection features, leading to improved thermal management and reliability. Both types are crucial for advancing electrification in vehicles and optimizing performance in electric and hybrid applications.</p></p>
