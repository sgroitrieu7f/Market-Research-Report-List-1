<p><p>The Special Fiber Optic Cutting Knife market is divided into two main types: Mechanical Fiber Optic Cutting Knives and Laser Fiber Optic Cutting Knives. Mechanical Fiber Optic Cutting Knives use physical blades to achieve precise cuts in optical fibers, making them suitable for various applications requiring manual operation. In contrast, Laser Fiber Optic Cutting Knives utilize high-energy laser beams for cutting, offering enhanced precision and reduced risk of fiber damage, ideal for automated processes and high-volume production environments.</p></p>
