<p><p>The digital duplicators market features two primary types: all-in-one duplicators and single-function duplicators. All-in-one duplicators combine multiple functions, such as printing, copying, and scanning, into a single machine, offering versatility for various tasks. Single-function duplicators, on the other hand, are specialized devices focused solely on printing, providing efficiency for high-volume document reproduction. Both types cater to different user needs, with all-in-one solutions appealing to those requiring multifunctionality and single-function devices serving organizations prioritizing speed and simplicity in printing tasks.</p></p>
