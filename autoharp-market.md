<p><p>The autoharp market can be categorized into three main types based on string material: cauda equina, nylon, and steel wire strings. Cauda equina strings, traditionally made from animal gut, offer warm tones and a unique timbre. Nylon strings are popular for their durability and softer sound, making them ideal for beginners. Steel wire strings produce a bright, strong tone favored by advanced players and in various music genres. Each type caters to specific preferences in sound quality and playing style.</p></p>
