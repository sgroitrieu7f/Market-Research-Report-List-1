<p><p>Cloud Services Brokerage (CSB) encompasses two primary market types: Internal Brokerage Enablement and External Brokerage Enablement. Internal Brokerage Enablement refers to organizations creating their own brokerage solutions to manage and integrate cloud services for internal users, enhancing efficiency and governance. In contrast, External Brokerage Enablement involves third-party brokers offering cloud services and solutions to end-users, facilitating access to a variety of cloud options and optimizing resource utilization while often providing additional support and management capabilities.</p></p>
