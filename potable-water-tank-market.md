<p><p>The potable water tank market includes three main types: GRP (Glass Reinforced Plastic) tanks, plastic tanks, and metal tanks. GRP tanks are lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and ideal for both above-ground and underground storage, offering durability and insulation. Plastic tanks are cost-effective, versatile, and resistant to chemicals, commonly used for residential and commercial applications. Metal tanks, often made of steel or aluminum, provide strength and longevity, suitable for large-scale storage but may require coatings to prevent rust and corrosion.</p></p>
