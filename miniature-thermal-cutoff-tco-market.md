<p><p>Miniature Thermal Cutoff (TCO) devices are crucial safety components used to prevent overheating in electronic products. The market for TCOs is segmented into two main types: single-use and resettable. </p><p>Single-use TCOs permanently disconnect the circuit after overheating, requiring replacement. In contrast, resettable TCOs can be manually reset after tripping, allowing for continuous use without replacement. This differentiation caters to varying consumer needs based on product design, longevity, and maintenance preferences, ultimately influencing market dynamics and demand patterns.</p></p>
