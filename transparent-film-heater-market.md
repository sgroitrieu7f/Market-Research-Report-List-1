<p><p>The transparent film heater market features two primary types: wire wound transparent heaters and ITO (Indium Tin Oxide) transparent heaters. Wire wound transparent heaters utilize wire elements embedded within a flexible film, offering efficient heat distribution for applications like automotive defrosting and touchscreens. ITO transparent heaters, on the other hand, employ a transparent conductive coating, making them ideal for use in displays and architectural heating where visibility is essential. Both types cater to various industries requiring transparency alongside heating capabilities.</p></p>
