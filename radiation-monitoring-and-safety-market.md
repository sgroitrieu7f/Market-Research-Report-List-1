<p><p>Radiation Monitoring and Safety Market encompasses various technologies for detecting radiation. Gas-filled detectors, such as Geiger-Muller counters, utilize ionization of gas to measure radiation levels. Scintillators employ materials that emit light when exposed to radiation, making them effective for detecting gamma and beta particles. Semiconductor-based detectors, like silicon and germanium detectors, offer high precision and energy resolution for radiation measurement. Other types may include dosimeters and neutron detectors, catering to specific monitoring applications in healthcare, nuclear, and environmental sectors.</p></p>
