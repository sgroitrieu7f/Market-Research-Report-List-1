<p><p>The bio-implants market includes three main types: allografts, autografts, and xenografts. Allografts involve tissue taken from a donor of the same species, suitable for various medical applications. Autografts utilize a patient's own tissue, offering the advantage of reduced rejection risk. Xenografts consist of tissue sourced from different species, often used in unique surgical scenarios. Each type has specific advantages and applications, catering to diverse medical needs, including orthopedic, dental, and reconstructive procedures.</p></p>
