<p><p>The pediatric imaging equipment market encompasses various types, each serving specific diagnostic needs. Ultrasound utilizes sound waves for imaging soft tissues, making it safe for infants. Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) offers detailed images of internal structures without radiation, ideal for brain and spinal assessments. Computed Tomography (CT) combines X-ray images for comprehensive views, useful in emergencies. Conventional X-ray provides quick assessments of bones and lungs. Other modalities may include fluoroscopy and nuclear medicine, catering to diverse pediatric imaging requirements.</p></p>
