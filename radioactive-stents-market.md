<p><p>Radioactive stents are specialized medical devices used primarily in the treatment of obstructive lesions in blood vessels and bile ducts. Internal (Double-J) stents facilitate urine flow between the kidney and bladder. Iodine-125 radioactive stents utilize radioactive isotopes to prevent tumor regrowth.
