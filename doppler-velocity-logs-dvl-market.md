<p><p>Doppler Velocity Logs (DVL) are advanced instruments used primarily in underwater applications to measure the velocity of a vessel relative to the seabed. Utilizing the Doppler effect, these devices send out sound waves that bounce off the sea floor and return, allowing for precise calculations of movement in three-dimensional space. DVLs are vital for marine navigation, underwater robotics, and autonomous vehicles, as they provide essential data for positioning and guidance in environments where GPS signals are unavailable.</p><p>The Doppler Velocity Logs (DVL) Market is anticipated to grow significantly, driven by increasing demand for underwater exploration, advancements in robotics, and expanding maritime activities.
