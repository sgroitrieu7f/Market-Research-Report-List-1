<p><p>The Wafer Analyzers market encompasses various types, including On-Wafer Microwave Measurement and De-embedding. On-Wafer Microwave Measurement involves testing and characterizing devices directly on the wafer, enabling precise performance analysis of high-frequency components before packaging. De-embedding refers to the process of isolating the true device characteristics by removing the effects of the test fixtures and parasitics from the measurement, ensuring accurate data interpretation. Together, these methodologies enhance the efficiency and accuracy of semiconductor device testing in R&D and manufacturing.</p></p>
