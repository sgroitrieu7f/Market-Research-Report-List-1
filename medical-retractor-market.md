<p><p>The medical retractor market is primarily divided into hand-held and self-retaining retractors. Hand-held retractors require a surgeon's assistance to maintain tissue visibility during procedures, providing flexibility and control. In contrast, self-retaining retractors are designed to hold tissue open on their own, allowing for hands-free operations, which can enhance surgical efficiency. Both types are essential in various surgical specialties, facilitating improved access to surgical sites and reducing fatigue for the surgical team.</p></p>
