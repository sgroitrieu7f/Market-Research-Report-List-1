<p><p>The Commercial Vehicle Anti-lock Braking Systems (ABS) market consists of various types based on the number of channels: </p><p>1. **Four Channels ABS** manages each wheel independently for optimal control, enhancing safety during heavy braking.</p><p>2. **Three Channels ABS** controls front wheels separately while managing rear wheels together, balancing efficiency and cost.</p><p>3. **Two Channels ABS** handles front and rear wheels as pairs, offering basic anti-lock functions.</p><p>4. **Single Channel ABS** focuses on a single axle system, providing minimal braking assistance. Each type offers different benefits in performance and safety.</p></p>
