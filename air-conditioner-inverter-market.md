<p><ul><li>Half - Bridge Inverter</li><li>Full - Bridge Inverter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air conditioner inverter market is primarily categorized into half-bridge and full-bridge inverters. Half-bridge inverters utilize two switches to control power conversion, making them more cost-effective and compact, suitable for smaller systems. Conversely, full-bridge inverters employ four switches, offering higher efficiency and better performance for larger, more demanding units. These inverter types are essential for regulating compressor speed, improving energy efficiency, and enhancing overall cooling performance in various air conditioning applications.</p></p>
