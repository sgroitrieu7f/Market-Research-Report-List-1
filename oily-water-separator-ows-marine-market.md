<p><p>Oily Water Separators (OWS) are essential marine equipment used to separate oil from wastewater generated on ships, ensuring compliance with environmental regulations. The market features several types: </p><p>1. **Gravity Plate Separator** uses gravity to separate oil from water based on density differences. </p><p>2. **Electrochemical Separator** employs electrical currents to enhance separation efficiency. </p><p>3. **Bioremediation** utilizes microorganisms to break down hydrocarbons. </p><p>4. **Centrifugal Separators** leverage high-speed rotation to effectively separate oil from water, providing precise treatment solutions. </p></p>
