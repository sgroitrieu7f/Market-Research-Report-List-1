<p><strong>The Confocal Chromatic Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Displacement Sensor</li><li>Thickness Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Confocal chromatic sensors are pivotal in precise measurement applications, categorized into displacement sensors and thickness sensors. Displacement sensors utilize confocal chromatic technology to measure the distance between the sensor and an object, providing high-resolution data for position tracking. In contrast, thickness sensors are designed to assess the thickness of materials by evaluating light reflection from both surfaces of an object. These sensors enable accurate and non-contact measurements, enhancing quality control across various industries, including manufacturing and materials science.</p></p>
