<p><ul><li>Immersed Aerators</li><li>Surface Aerators</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water treatment aerators market includes three main types: immersed aerators, surface aerators, and others. Immersed aerators are submerged in water, effectively enhancing oxygen transfer rates for biological processes. Surface aerators, installed at the water's surface, utilize mechanical means to agitate water and introduce air, making them ideal for wastewater treatment ponds. The 'others' category encompasses specialized aeration systems, such as diffused aerators and advanced technologies, catering to diverse industrial and municipal applications, enhancing efficiency in water treatment processes.</p></p>
