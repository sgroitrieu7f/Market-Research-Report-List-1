<p><strong>The Leather Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solventborne</li><li>Waterborne</li><li>UV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The leather coatings market comprises three main types: solventborne, waterborne, and UV-cured coatings. Solventborne coatings use organic solvents for application, offering excellent adhesion and durability, but raise environmental concerns. Waterborne coatings, which utilize water as a solvent, are more eco-friendly and minimize volatile organic compounds, though they may require longer drying times. UV-cured coatings leverage ultraviolet light for instant drying and hardening, providing a durable finish with reduced solvent emissions, making them increasingly popular in sustainable practices.</p></p>
