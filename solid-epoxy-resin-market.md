<p><p>The solid epoxy resin market is segmented into low, medium, and high viscosity types based on their fluidity and application suitability. Low viscosity epoxy resin is ideal for applications requiring deep penetration and wetting, commonly used in coatings and adhesives. Medium viscosity resin is versatile, suitable for a variety of industrial uses, including composites and bonding agents. High viscosity epoxy resin is typically employed in thicker applications, providing excellent gap-filling properties and structural strength, often used in construction and automotive sectors.</p></p>
