<p><p>The Track Geometry Measurement System (TGMS) is essential for assessing railway track conditions to ensure safety and efficiency. The market is divided into two primary types: Non-Contact Based TGMS, which uses technologies like lasers and cameras to evaluate track geometry without physical contact, providing high accuracy and efficiency; and Contact Based TGMS, which relies on physical sensors and devices that make direct contact with the track, allowing for detailed measurements but potentially impacting track integrity. Both types play crucial roles in railway maintenance and management.</p></p>
