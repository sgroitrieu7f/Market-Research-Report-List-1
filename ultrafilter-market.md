<p><p>Ultrafilter market types primarily include Tube Type and Roll Type ultrafilters. Tube Type ultrafilters consist of cylindrical membrane modules, often utilized in applications requiring high pressure and durability, such as water treatment and chemical processing. In contrast, Roll Type ultrafilters feature membrane rolls designed for compact systems, offering efficient space usage and flexibility in various filtration tasks. Both types serve critical roles in separating particles, enhancing product quality, and ensuring compliance in industries like food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and wastewater management.</p></p>
