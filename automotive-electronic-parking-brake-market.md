<p><p>The Automotive Electronic Parking Brake (EPB) market is categorized based on regulatory standards: FMVSS 105, FMVSS 135, and ECE 13H. FMVSS 105 pertains to the performance of brake systems in vehicles, ensuring safety and reliability. FMVSS 135 focuses on the performance criteria for passenger cars, emphasizing effective braking under various conditions. ECE 13H is a European standard governing the installation and performance of parking brakes in vehicles, ensuring compliance with safety and operational efficiency. Each type impacts design and technology advancements in EPB systems.</p></p>
